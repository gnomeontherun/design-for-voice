# Resources

Want to learn about voice design and development? Here is a list of some of the resources recommended on the show or by the host.

## Websites about Design

* [Google Conversational Design Site](https://designguidelines.withgoogle.com/conversation/)
* [Alexa Design](https://alexa.design)
* [Alexa Design Guide](https://developer.amazon.com/docs/alexa-design/get-started.html)
* [Designing for Conversation Course](https://developer.amazon.com/ask-resources/guided/conversational-design-workshop#/)
* [Voxable Blog](https://www.voxable.io/blog)
